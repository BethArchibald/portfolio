About Me
I'm a self-taught motion graphics designer with a background in teaching. I am inspired by the power of motion graphics and animation to stir our emotions, transform our outlooks on the world and bring ideas to life.


I know from my own past experience teaching primary school children how powerful and transformative animation can be in capturing imaginations and broadening perspectives.


Skills: After Effects, Illustrator, Photoshop, Premiere Pro, HTML, CSS and basic web development skills. Fluent speaker of German and five-aside footballer.
